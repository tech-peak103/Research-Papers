Programme / 01

Two disciplines.

We host two distinct tracks of international scholarly competition. Submit a rigorously researched paper, or compose an essay of literary merit. Each is reviewed by a rotating panel of fellows.

Track 01
↗

Research Papers

Original empirical or theoretical work, 4,000–12,000 words. Blind reviewed by two members of the international panel of fellows.
Track 02
↗

Essay Competitions

Themed prompts on philosophy, literature, ethics, and the life of ideas. Judged on argument, prose, and originality of thought.
